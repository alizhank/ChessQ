About us

The oldest currently operating chess club in the world is the Zurich Chess Club, founded in 1809.

In 1953, the club organized a chess candidates tournament in which participants competed for the right to play in a match for the title of world chess champion. The first German chess club was the Berlin Schadows Schachklub (existed from 1803 to 1847), the oldest currently operating German chess clubs are the Berlin club Berliner Schachgesellschaft 1827 Eckbauer, founded in 1827 and Hamburg Hamburger SK (founded in 1830).

One of the oldest chess clubs in London was the St. George Chess Club (founded in 1843). The oldest university chess club in the UK is the Oxford University Chess Club, founded in 1869. One of the first American chess clubs were Mechanics' Institute Chess Club, Marshall Chess Club, Manhattan Chess Club. In Russia, the first chess club was the St. Petersburg Society of Chess Lovers (opened in 1853).
