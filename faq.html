Some common questions
were often asked

With what level of knowledge can I join the club?

Everyone can join our club, from beginners to professionals

How much does club membership cost?

We have 3 plans, one of them is absolutely free, which includes only basic privileges

Is it possible to come to the club with pets?

We love pets, so you can come with your pet

Do you have any coffee?

We have a bar in the club, you can have a snack there, also make coffee

Do you communicate with each other in Kazakh?

There are many people of different nationalities in our club, we use English for communication, but we also teach Kazakh

What is the maximum number of members in your club?

currently, we have 125 members, we are constantly expanding, so we plan to reach 200 members
